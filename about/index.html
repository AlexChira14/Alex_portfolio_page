About me

Born and raised in Bilbao, Spain. Graduated in Mathematics from the University of the Basque Country in Spain, with a special mention in Applied Mathematics, Statistics and Computing. Since I was a child, I have always been attracted to the use of Mathematics to solve real life issues, using logic, reasoning and abstract thinking. Programming has been my favourite subject as an important problem-solving tool.

Within my predilection for Applied Mathematics, I feel especially attracted by Big Data and Machine Learning. I completed a summer course in Denmark which was my first window into this field. I have also attended to several Data Science related courses in my free time, where I have learned SQL, Machine Learning algorithms, Python programming focused on Data Science, data analysis, Deep Learning, Google Cloud Engineering and MLOps.

I had the chance to start my professional career at Endesa during an internship of a whole year, although after 6 months they offered me some important positions inside the firm after excellent feedback of my work. Finally, I joined to the Customer Care area, where I continue working as a data scientist but in closer contact with the business needs. Then I moved to Dentsu where I am developing marketing related models to specific clients with a great data team. In both companies the focus on the business needs are key points for successfully performing the work, apart from the technical aspects. Meanwhile I continue my ML education with some well-valued online courses. Thanks to these positions, my travels, sports carried out and associations for which I have collaborated with, I have also been able to see the importance of teamwork and how significant it is to learn from others to be a better partner.

During my working experience I have developed and used various data science tools:

  • Time-serie predictions: ARIMA, SARIMAX, RNN
  • Machine Learning classification and regression algorithms (Random Forest, XGBoost, SVM, clustering, Linear/Logistic Regression…)
  • Use of principal Data Science frameworks and packages for Python: Pandas, Numpy, Matplotly, Seaborn, Scikit-learn, Scipy, Keras, Tensorflow…
  • Google Cloud Platform: BigQuery, VertexAI, Data Studio…
  • Deep Learning: CNN, RNN, hyperparameter tuning…
  • SQL: MySQL, PostgreSQL
  • Proficiency in Excel, PowerPoint, Access…
  • Version control tools: Git

Regarding the languages, I am a native Spanish speaker, and I am also proficient in Basque and English. I do speak a bit of French.
